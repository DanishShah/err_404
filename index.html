What it's about?

ERR_404 is a 24-hour Hackathon organized by M.H.Saboo Siddik College of Engineering to provide a platform to all the hackers to showcase their talents.
M.H.S.S.C.O.E calls out all the coders, programmers & hackers to come together,showcase your skills,network with your peers & build an awesome software project in a competitive & healthy enviornment.
The hackathon will run across the weekend which offers students the chance to split into groups, brainstorm project ideas, and create a working prototype before the time limit is up!
Whether it's your first hackathon, or your 50th, we'd love to see students of all skill levels and from all colleges come along for a weekend of creativity, collaboration and code.
Our major goal is to bring everyone together and have a fun weekend :)
The sky's the limit!!!

General Guidelines

FAQ's


Who can participate?

Anyone with a knack of programming and solving problems can participate.

How much does it cost?

Thanks to our college,attendance is completely free.We will also provide you with WiFi,dinner and a workspace.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop,charger and other gadgets suitable to your project and a will to code.Please bring a valid ID for admission.

Where is it?

M.H.Saboo Siddik College of Engineering,Byculla is happy to provide space in their campus to organize the Hackathon.You can find the directions in the Venue section.

Where will I sleep?

We have sleeping area with matresses and pillows.You can bring along sleeping bags & other stuffs.

I don't have a team );

Don't worry! Part of the fun of a hackathon is meeting new people. We will have time at the beginning of the event to socialize and form teams.
